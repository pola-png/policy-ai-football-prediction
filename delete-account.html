Football Prediction App

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please read this page carefully before proceeding.

How to Delete Your Account

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the Football Prediction App
  2. Go to Profile & Settings (tap the profile icon)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion when prompted
  5. Your account will be deleted immediately

Method 2: Email Request

  1. Send an email to: ezekielxap@gmail.com
  2. Subject: "Account Deletion Request - Football Prediction App"
  3. Include your username and email address used in the app
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days
  5. You will receive confirmation once deletion is complete

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and profile data
  • Chat Messages: All community chat messages and prediction comments
  • Usage Data: App interaction history and analytics data
  • VIP Subscriptions: Purchase history and tier access
  • Preferences: All app settings and customizations

Data Retention

  • Immediate Deletion: Account data, messages, and personal information
  • 30-Day Retention: Backup copies for system recovery (then permanently deleted)
  • Legal Compliance: Some transaction records may be retained for tax/legal requirements as mandated by law
Note: After account deletion, you will lose access to all VIP subscriptions and cannot recover any data. Refunds are not provided for unused VIP access.
